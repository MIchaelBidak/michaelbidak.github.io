How It Works

A search, start to finish

Here’s the honest version of what working together looks like. We start with a short call — you tell me about the role, the team, and what’s made this hire tricky. If I can help, I build the search around your bar, not a keyword list. If I can’t, I’ll tell you that on the call and point you somewhere better.

From there, my whole job is keeping the search off your plate: sourcing, screening conversations, scheduling, feedback loops, and the offer itself. And there’s no black box — you’ll always know exactly where the search stands, who’s in the pipeline, and what candidates are telling me.

  • Role calibration & search strategy | Week one
  • First calibrated shortlist | Days, not weeks

Specialties

Startups

Your first engineers set the ceiling for everything that comes after. I help founders hire people who can build without a playbook — and who genuinely want to. When you’re making hire number three, there’s no room for a “pretty good fit.”

Specialty Areas: founding engineers | product‑minded builders | generalists | first eng hires

Scale‑ups

You found something that works, and now everyone needs to hire twelve people by next quarter. I build pipelines that keep pace with your growth without letting the bar slip — because the fastest way to slow down is to hire fast and badly.

Specialty Areas: team buildouts | Staff+ ICs | new managers | infrastructure

Enterprise

Big companies don’t struggle to get applicants — they struggle to find the three people on Earth with the exact niche skills who aren’t looking. Reaching those three, quietly and convincingly, is my favorite kind of search.

Specialty Areas: niche skills | confidential searches | hard‑to‑fill reqs | leadership

Candidates

Job hunting is great — rejection emails are so warm and personal! Wouldn’t that be nice. If you’re an engineer quietly wondering what’s next, I’ll represent you the way I’d want to be represented — and your résumé never goes anywhere without your say‑so.

Specialty Areas: confidential representation | career‑goal mapping | comp & offer guidance | honest feedback

How I vet

Technical Calibration

I’m not an engineer, and I won’t pretend to be one. Instead, I calibrate the technical bar with your best engineers before the search starts — so every candidate is measured against a standard that’s yours, not mine.

Career‑Goal Alignment

I match on growth, not just compensation. When a move fits where someone is actually trying to go — not just what they’re paid to do today — they stay. That’s the whole reason my placements stick.

Human Screening

Every candidate gets a real conversation with me before you ever see a résumé. Not a chatbot, not a form — a call where I listen for the things a profile can’t tell you.
