Why a human headhunter?

A headhunter is only as good as the human behind the search. Software has made it easy to send a thousand messages — and impossible to feel seen by any of them. An algorithm can rank a résumé, but it can’t hear the hesitation in a candidate’s voice, or catch the spark when someone describes the work they love. That part doesn’t automate. My job is to know great engineers before you need them — and to know your team well enough to recognize the one who’ll thrive in it.
