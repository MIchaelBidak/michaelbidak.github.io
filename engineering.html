How it works

Three steps, no black box. You’ll always know exactly where the search stands.

We talk about the role, the team, and what’s made this search tricky. No pitch, no deck. If I can help, I’ll tell you exactly how. If I can’t, I’ll point you to someone who can, free of charge, free of hard feelings.

I sit down with you and your best engineers to define what great looks like, then go find it. That means sourcing far beyond the apply button and having a real conversation with every candidate before you ever see a name.

You get a short, calibrated list, not a stack of maybes. I handle scheduling, feedback loops, and the offer conversation, and I stay close until the start date, and well past it.
